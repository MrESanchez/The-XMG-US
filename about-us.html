WHO WE ARE

X Markets Group is a globally based company providing 5G mobile wireless broadband focusing on high-speed internet connection while decreasing costs with the help of advanced software and hardware technologies. Founded in 1999, X Markets Group is based in London, New York and San Francisco.

We are a Minority-Owned Business Enterprise corporation with over twenty related patents pending.

XMG’s Radio Wave Technology can make New York City the world’s first metropolis entirely covered by fast and reliable Wi-Fi.

With coverage so expansive, Wi-Fi access can be a reality from anywhere in this smart city, where everyone can get online.

Due to the low costs of radio wave technology that requires little to no maintenance, the service costs will be minimal. Virtually all low-income houses can get it for free.

The biggest advantage of XMG’s Radio Wave Technology is its high speed. With bandwidth and speeds reaching from 600 Megabits to one Gigabit, this means XMG will provide the highest performance amongst all other network service providers in the entirety of New York City.

With “One World Trade Center”, the tallest building in the United States, being the main center of antennas in the region, it will be at the very center of the point-to-point mesh.

The Infrastructure is designed to be shared by multiple service providers, and competition is never curtailed due to network capacity, physical space in network hub locations, or technology choices.

OUR VISION


XMG is a globally based company that prides itself on Radio Wave Technology and microwave-based services, that has now started to use its technology for high quality, high-speed internet while also decreasing the cost with the help of advanced hardware and software technologies.

Here at XMG, we believe that all children should have a way to dream and learn, no matter what their station in life; they all deserve a chance to obtain an endless amount of knowledge and education right to their door. Underprivileged families can be given a new lease on life, with the world at their fingertips; they will finally have access to enriching information through our advanced Wireless Access Technologies.

No one will face a barrier based on who they are or where they live. The infrastructure has capabilities throughout the city, able to reach all residents and businesses everywhere, outdoors, and indoors.

Working closely with the New York City Economic Development Corporation to train underprivileged youth, XMG has already broadened job specifications for the brightest talent of the future.

CHARITY AIMS

Our company, has and always will, pride themselves on helping those less fortunate get that extra bit that they need in order to have a better, more equal life. That’s why we continuously search out new ways in which we can help people and their communities and a great way of doing that is through good, honest, hardworking charity organisations.
